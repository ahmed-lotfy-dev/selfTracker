## Design Context

### Users
Self-trackers — people who log workouts, weight, habits, nutrition, and tasks to improve themselves. Used daily during workouts, meal times, and end-of-day reflection. Mobile-first, on-the-go usage.

### Brand Personality
Disciplined, rewarding, vibrant. 3 words: **earnest, energetic, refined**. Should feel like a premium fitness companion — not a clinical tracker, not a gamified gimmick.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Dark-first with a refined light mode
- **Primary:** Emerald green (#10B981 / #34D399) — growth, health, vitality
- **Vibe:** Clean, card-based with deep backgrounds and crisp borders. Premium without being heavy. Think WHOOP meets Linear — data-rich but serene.
- **Typography:** Bold headings (font-black), clean body. System font for performance.
- **Motion:** Spring-based micro-interactions on press/enter, none gratuitous.
- **Anti-references:** No neon on dark, no gradient text, no side-stripe card accents, no glassmorphism.
- **Dark mode:** True dark with layered charcoal surfaces (#14161A → #1C1F26 → #232733).

### Design Principles
1. **Data first** — Numbers are the hero. Cards should surface key metrics immediately.
2. **Calm confidence** — The UI should feel premium and trustworthy, never toy-like. Ample whitespace, deliberate typography.
3. **Motion with purpose** — Spring animations on interactions (press, appear) to feel responsive. No decorative auto-play.
4. **Emerald as signal** — Green is used for primary actions AND success states. Don't overuse — let it keep its meaning.
5. **Consistent rhythm** — 4pt spacing scale, rounded-2xl on cards, rounded-xl on inputs/buttons. Every component belongs to the same family.

# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Gym-goers and fitness-minded people who track workouts, weight, habits, nutrition, and tasks daily. Used on-the-go during gym sessions, meal times, and end-of-day check-ins. Mobile-first Android with future iOS expansion.

## Product Purpose

A premium yet simple personal tracker for the gym crowd — log workouts, weight, habits, nutrition, and tasks in one place. Local-first with sync, published on Google Play and eventually the App Store. Designed to feel like a refined companion, not another generic fitness app.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, simplistic, refined.

## Anti-references

Should not look like any other fitness app or gym site. Avoid the typical fitness-app aesthetic — no aggressive neon, no bodybuilder imagery, no cluttered dashboards. Must feel distinct and premium, not generic.

## Design Principles

1. **Keyboard-aware** — Inputs must always be visible when the keyboard is open on Android. Never let the keyboard obscure the field being typed into.
2. **Fit the flow** — The app is used mid-workout and on-the-go. Every screen must load fast and require minimal taps to log data.
3. **Refined simplicity** — Strip everything that isn't essential. Premium comes from restraint, not decoration.
4. **Data at a glance** — The home screen should surface key metrics immediately without scrolling or tapping.
5. **Confident motion** — Spring micro-interactions on press, but never decorative auto-play. Motion should feel responsive, not gratuitous.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA contrast ratios
- Support reduced motion (fade instead of spring)
- Touch targets ≥44x44px for mobile
- Ensure inputs are never hidden by the Android soft keyboard
